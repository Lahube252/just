About JUST

The History Of Jamhuriya University

Jamhuriya University of Science and Technology (JUST) is a nonprofit and private institution. It was officially established in Mogadishu, Somalia, in 2011 by a group of Somali scholars and Intellectuals. The main drive of founding JUST is to fill the existing gap (of quality) in the higher education system in Somalia by providing a high quality higher education of international standard.!
